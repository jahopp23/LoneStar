Loan Process

1. Submit Loan Application & Supporting Documentation

  • Lone Star Application
  • Fully Executed Sales Contract
  • Itemized Rehab Budget
  • Last Two Bank Statements

2. Lone Star will Underwrite the Property Value

  • We will issue preliminary rates and loan amount
  • Upon agreement, borrower pays $600 underwriting fee
  • Lender and Borrower agree on final terms

3. Close the Deal

  • Provide Title Commitment and Builder's Risk Insurance Certificate
  • Congratulations!
